Important: AI Limitations & Your Control

AI can make confident mistakes. It does not understand your life—it only works with words. You are the final authority on your own decisions.

  • Verify — AI can generate plausible nonsense. Trust your own judgment.
  • Stop anytime — If a response feels wrong, disengage. Your intuition is valid.
  • No obligation — You are not required to follow, accept, or even read what AI generates.
  • Dignity first — If any response compromises your dignity, discard it immediately.

These templates are invitations, not commands. The system serves you. You do not serve the system.

Universal Stop Conditions

You may stop at any time, for any reason, without explanation.

• Your dignity is compromised — yours or anyone else's
• You are pressured to decide without enough information
• Your options for leaving the situation are being blocked
• The process becomes too much for you right now
